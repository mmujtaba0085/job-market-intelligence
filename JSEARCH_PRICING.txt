- 5 requests/min
- 50 requests/day
- ❌ Too slow for weekly pipeline

BASIC TIER ($9.99/month):
- 100 requests/min
- 5,000 requests/month
- ✓ Good for 1-2 markets

PRO TIER ($29.99/month):
- 500 requests/min
- 50,000 requests/month
- ✓✓ Excellent for multiple markets

ULTRA TIER ($99.99/month):
- 2,000 requests/min
- 500,000 requests/month
- ✓✓✓ Enterprise scale

RECOMMENDATION:
For your current setup (1 market, 9 keywords × 4 countries = 36 requests/week):
→ Basic tier ($9.99/mo) is sufficient
→ Allows ~138 requests per day
→ Can expand to 3-4 markets easily
